Method notes

  • Source: NOAA GML monthly mean CO2 dataset for Mauna Loa.
  • Refresh: The app fetches the live NOAA text feed every time the page loads.
  • Model: Additive Holt-Winters with 12-month seasonality.
  • Parameter fit: Grid search chooses alpha, beta, and gamma that minimize one-step-ahead SSE.
  • Prediction intervals: 95% bounds are derived from one-step residual variance and widen with forecast horizon.
  • Missing or negative monthly mean values are excluded before fitting.
